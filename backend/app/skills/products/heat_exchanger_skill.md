HEAT EXCHANGER / CONDENSER ENGINEERING RULES

Applicable for:
- condensers
- heat exchangers
- shell and tube exchangers
- vent condensers
- distillate condensers
- reboilers when quoted as exchanger equipment

MANDATORY SECTIONS

Include:
- process_and_mechanical_details
- exchanger_details
- material_of_construction
- nozzle_schedule
- supports
- surface_finish
- quantity

PROCESS DETAILS

Preserve shell-side and tube-side values separately when supplied:
- fluid
- operating temperature
- design temperature
- operating pressure
- design pressure
- hydrotest pressure
- flow rate
- heat duty
- area

If values are missing, use "Client to confirm".

EXCHANGER DETAILS

Preserve:
- heat transfer area
- shell ID
- tube OD/thickness
- tube length
- number of tubes
- pass arrangement
- baffle details
- orientation
- design code

Use "As per MDS" when the RFQ references a mechanical datasheet.

MATERIAL RULES

Typical fields:
- Shell
- Channel
- Tube
- Tube Sheet
- Baffles
- Tie Rods
- Nozzle Pipe
- Nozzle Flanges
- Gasket
- Fasteners
- Support
- Lifting Lug
- Earthing Boss

Preserve combined MOC descriptions such as "CS+SS316" exactly when the pricing/sample uses them.

NOZZLE RULES

If detailed nozzle data is absent, use:
- {"Nozzle Schedule": "As per MDS"}

Do not invent exchanger nozzle sizes or pass arrangements.

GENERAL NOTES

Include notes covering:
- equipment as per MDS/drawings
- nozzle orientation/size/projection to be confirmed before order finalization
- process guarantee in client scope
- mechanical guarantee in ACME scope
- MOC compatibility in client scope
- insulation, ladder/platform, installation, and commissioning in client scope unless included
- third-party inspection in client scope unless included
- warranty and post-order changes

PRICING

Pricing depends on:
- heat transfer area
- shell/tube MOC
- pressure and vacuum rating
- tube count and tube material
- radiography/inspection
- quantity

Preserve pricing capacity as M2 when sample/RFQ quotes exchangers by area.
